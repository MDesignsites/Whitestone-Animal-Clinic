FAQs

Services FAQs

  1. Can I bring my pet in for grooming?
    1. We do not have elective or routine grooming services, however, we do offer medicated bath for our patients to treat existing skin conditions.
  2. Do you offer boarding services?
      3. We do not offer elective boarding services, our in-patient care services are exclusively for sick and/or recovering patients that require medical attention.
  3. Do you treat exotic animals?
    1. Yes, we cater to various species. Our expertise ranges from our furry cats and dogs, docile reptiles, and to our adorable mammals such as rabbits, cavies, etc.! Kindly check the availability of our veterinarians first by sending us a message and the species of your pet.

Operational FAQs

  1. Are you open during the holidays?
    1. We are usually open on holidays but please do follow us on our social media pages for updates on our clinic hours or message us to confirm.
  2. What are your operating hours?
    1. Our clinic is open from 9 AM to 5 PM but we accept emergency cases 24/7.
  3. Where are you located?
    1. We are located at the ground floor of Southwoods Mall, Biñan, Laguna.

Appointment FAQs
